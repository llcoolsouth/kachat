When John Bishop and Lucas Southworth met in the second grade, John said, "Hello, I am John. Would you like to do a podcast with me?" Lucas replied, "Of course, but only if it about the Disney/Pixar series of movies about talking cars that will exist in about four years." The rest, they say, is history. After 15 years of preparation, they released the first episode of The Ka-Chat. In this podcast, they explore the movies Cars, Cars 2, Cars 3, Planes, and Planes 2: Fire and Rescue, answering questions like "If there is a Car Pope, what is Car Catholicism like?", "Where do little baby Cars come from?", and "Why is the Cars 2 video game so good?" (Although this is a podcast about a series of children's movies, we use a fair amount profanity and discuss some mature topics, so use your judgement before you let your five year old listen to us ramble.)
