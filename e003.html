Episode 3: Race-Ism

Released ##/##/###

Strap in, folks. Today's episode gets a little racey. By that, I mean in this episode, we talk about the concept of race and ethnicity within the Cars universe, establishing that race does in fact exist in this universe, and along with it, racism. The implications of prejudice and hate based on race in a world full of anthropomorphized Cars are many, so let's go ahead and get into it. Oh, also we go on a five minute tangent dealing with a 13 second clip from Cars 2 that has nothing to do with our current topic. So, pretty par for the course, really.
